Meet Our Founder

Roshan Gupta

As someone who grew up experiencing the warmth and unity of community gatherings around food, I was inspired to create BhandaraChalu. The idea came from seeing how technology could bridge the gap between those seeking spiritual nourishment and the temples offering prasad through bhandaras. Our mission is to ensure that this beautiful tradition of sharing food continues to thrive in our digital age.

Our Vision

To ensure that no one goes hungry by making free food events (Bhandaras) easily discoverable. We aim to blend tradition with technology, promoting a culture of seva, unity, and compassion across India.

Our Mission

To build a user-friendly platform that connects people with real-time community food events near temples and spiritual centers. Through BhandaraChalu, we aim to create awareness, increase participation, and strengthen community bonds.
